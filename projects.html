  • Project 2:

    • In a recent project, I created a basic 3D model prototype for a consumer product.
    Using entry-level 3D modeling software, I designed and sculpted a simplified representation of the product,
    focusing on its key features and overall form. While the model lacked intricate details,
    it served as an effective visual aid for concept validation and initial design exploration.
    The prototype enabled stakeholders to visualize the product's dimensions and proportions,
    providing valuable insights for further iteration and refinement.
    This project demonstrated my ability to translate design concepts into tangible 3D representations,
    laying the groundwork for future development and refinement.
